SLAUGHTERDOME

Wave Survival Arena

The year is 2089. The old world burned in the Collapse -- wars, plagues, resource death. What rose from the ash wasn't civilization. It was spectacle.

THE DOME is the last arena standing. A circular kill-floor ringed by concrete and razor wire, broadcast to every surviving settlement on the net. Trees have reclaimed the edges. Toxic water pools seep through cracked earth as the rounds progress. The walls close in.

You are a condemned fighter. Your crimes don't matter. Your name doesn't matter. Only your kill count. Survive enough waves and the Dome grants you something no one else has -- another day alive.

No one has made it past wave 30.
